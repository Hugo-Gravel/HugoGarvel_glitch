Everyone in town knows Rwylon for one reason or another. Maybe they know him as 'That young fella at my burger joint that stomped a kid that was being a jackass for me' or 'Some odd cerulean that helped me escape some bullies' or 'The jackass that stomped on my morial!'. Regardless of the reason, almost everyone has heard his name or soon will! To the average troll, he is always ready with a snappy joke or a quick punch. He's gives a 'everyone's third best friend' feel and is always ready to help them out!
Personality (what're they like?)
Rwylon portrays himself as a... what do you call a golden boy, but on alternia... a rusty silver boy! He constantly finds himself in situations where me meets more people to help out or get into fights with. He doesn't care for most things, letting things slide and forgetting about them. If it's somthing too large to forget, he's likely to get into a rageful tone which often leads to fights. He lacks discipline, and focuses on using brute force or a silver tongue to get himself out of problems. If he isn't able to use humor or a compliment to get out of a problem, he'll lie or get into a brawl. He's constantly cracking jokes and smiling to those around him! When alone, Rwylon doesn't like to be a tough guy, and prefers to throw on Alternian-Princess movies (just imagine disney) and read really cute romance comics.
