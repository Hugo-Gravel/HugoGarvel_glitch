Backstory (what's their story?)
"Zahra had a pretty normal childhood growing up. School was a breeze for her, her parents were kind and loving, and her brother was fun to hang out with and pull a few tricks on. During this time, she met her close friend, Alex, who quickly became the sister that she never had. Several years passed, where Zahra would explore some of her own interests, and learn about the excitement of going to the range. One day, while Zahra had went to Alex's house for a sleepover, her brother Zeph had received a copy of SBURB and began to play it. Soon enough, Zahra's possessions, family, and even her home had disappeared, leaving only a mere crater behind where her house was, as well as the small shed in the backyard. Ever since SBURB had whisked away her family, Zahra has been searching through every single guidebook, website, and forum for a way to figure out what exactly had happened, and how in the world she could possibly find them. Until today, when she discovered a post online by LA."
Personality (what're they like?)
"Zahra is inquisitive, cunning and determined to reach the goals that she sets for herself. She wears her heart upon her sleeve, and isn't afraid to take risks. If a situation involves her friends or loved ones at stake, she would rather burn the world to the ground than lose her friends. She loves trying new things, even if she might not be the best at it initially. At her worst, Zahra is impulsive, a bit of a know-it-all, and judges herself harshly. She knows that she's got good intuition, and if no one is going to take action, she certainly will. She usually tries to keep a level head, but will often get emotional if the situation regards her friends or family, leading her to make rash decisions in the heat of the moment. She is her own worst critic in most situations if one of her plans goes awry."
Guardian (who raised them?)
"Zahra and her brother Zeph were raised by their kind and loving parents, Esmerelda and William Kenrith. Both of them are avid hunters and would take them to the range for occasional family trips, ensuring that their kids were knowledgeable in all of the rules of gun safety. Esmerelda works as a lawyer, and would often encourage her children to do the best that they could in school, valuing high achievement. Their father William works as a dental assistant, though he often would fiddle with gunsmithing on the side. No matter what, they placed the well-being of their children as their top priority, and did whatever they could to assist with their children's interests. Zahra loves her family, which is why she was extremely devastated when they had vanished."
Hobbies and Interests
""Thrifting: Even before your family had vanished, you would often go with Alex to see what kinds of interesting things you could find at the thrift store, even making a game out of who could make the most outrageous outfit. As of now, all of your clothing comes from the local thrift store. Sewing & Embroidery: You learned how to sew up a few small holes when you had discovered that a shirt that you bought had a small hole in it. As you went to the thrift store more frequently, you ended up learning how to sew a bit more formally. Gunslinging: Before your parents' disappearance, they used to go on family trips with you and your brother to the local range. You found that you had quite the talent for shooting, and you ended up getting hooked on it. You currently carry the gun that your father uses, the Browning Citora, at all times. Video Games: You would often ask Zeph if you could play with him, and though he had initially declined, he gave in. He played a lot of FPS and RPG games, and when he figured out that your gunslinging talent transferred pretty well to the digital scene, he started duoing with you for a lot of online tournaments. You even started looking for a few games that you liked on your own, and found that you enjoyed Pokemon as well as more strategy-based games. Reading: Your old room used to have a ton of books ranging from high-fantasy, to sci-fi, to even a few more historically accurate pieces. You loved spending hours and hours under the covers with a flashlight, reading up on all sorts of adventures. The Occult: You've consulted all sorts of mystical and occult texts trying to figure out if any of these might be able to assist in finding your family. Although they don't really seem to be that useful, you keep them with you, just in case." "
Reasons for Playing SBURB
"Your reasons for playing SBURB are simple. You just want to reunite with your family, and find out where the hell they even are. It was a Saturday when your life changed. Your parents were downstairs cooking up some breakfast, while your brother was upstairs playing that new game that had just released. He had been talking to you about it for months, and had hyped it up so much! You had packed up a few of your things, said your goodbyes and headed over to your friend Alex's house for a sleepover. Everything had gone fine, you hung out, played a few games, until the news reports of the meteors started. You had initially brushed it off, but once the reporter had mentiond ""SBURB"" you ran to your house in the dead of night, and came to see that the only thing that remained was a smoldering crater. You fell to your knees, in both sadness and disbelief, before passing out. When you awoke in the morning, you found yourself upon the couch at Alex's house, as she had trailed behind you when you left, and carried you back. The next day, you returned to the crater, and tried to search for any remaining remants of your family's existence. In doing so, you found that the shed in the backyard was seemingly untouched. You entered, and took everything, from the various gardening tools that you had made so many memories with your mother, to the excess of board games and books that were used on family game nights, as well as your father's Browning Citora. As time passed, you did more and more intensive researh, scouring any websites or guides that even mentioned SBURB. And now that you've found this link, it's time for you to find your family."
